Hello! Welcome to my home on the world wide web. I'm Tim and this is where I tell you a little about myself. I was born and raised in St. Louis, MO. I spent many years working at banks and financial companies and now I'm a developer in Louisville, KY.

So why did I make this site?

A couple years ago I was interested in learning more about web development, so I snooped around online and found all the free tutorials. I thought they were really cool and I learned some stuff, but I wanted to learn more. So I searched around again and I found Code Louisville, a 12-week software development course. I took a few courses there and I liked it so much that I wanted even more. That's when I quit my bank job and went to the Software Guild, a full-time software development bootcamp. There I learned about Java programming, HTML, CSS, MySQL, Spring MVC, and a bunch of other great buzz words that you can find on my resume.

I mainly made this site as way to get practice using HTML, CSS, and general front-end development tools.

Questions? Comments? General suggestions? Feel free to send me an email and will likely respond.
